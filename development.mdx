# Product

## Two-Tier User System

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Easy Mode" icon="sparkles">
    One-click liquidity vaults with predefined strategies and automation.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Pro Mode" icon="sliders">
    Manual range-based LP creation with analytics and simulations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Core Features

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Users connect and choose a strategy or build their own position.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Capital Deployment">
    Funds are allocated into vetted markets via vault contracts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automation Layer">
    Exit rules, alerts, and rebalancing protect capital.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Yield Collection">
    Users earn market fees plus optional incentives.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Differentiators

- AI risk scoring per market  
- Suggested probability ranges  
- Liquidity heatmaps  
- Simulation engine  
- Automated exit policies
