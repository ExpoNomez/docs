## Core Features

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Users connect and choose a strategy or build their own position.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capital Deployment">
    Funds are allocated into vetted markets via vault contracts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automation Layer">
    Exit rules, alerts, and rebalancing protect capital.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Yield Collection">
    Users earn market fees plus optional incentives.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Differentiators

- Suggested probability ranges
