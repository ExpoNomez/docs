# Flowlayer

## Liquidity Infrastructure for Prediction Markets

> Flowlayer is a non-custodial liquidity aggregation and optimization layer designed to make providing liquidity in prediction markets simple, capital-efficient, and scalable.

## What Flowlayer Does

Flowlayer aggregates user liquidity and deploys it into prediction markets using automated strategies, manual LP tools, and safety automation.

It transforms liquidity provision from a manual trading activity into a structured yield strategy.

<Info>
  ```mdx
  Flowlayer does not modify underlying market mechanics.  
  It acts as a coordination, optimization, and UX layer.
  ```
</Info>
